History

I was born and raised in Phoenix, Arizona. I went to high school in Scottsdale at Notre Dame Preparatory where I was first exposed to computer programming. I learned to make "Karel the Robot" programs, html webpages, and object models.

I am attending Northern Arizona University in the beautiful mountain city of Flagstaff. I immediately entered the Computer Science program and haven't looked back. Currently, I work for NAU's Green Fund as the Committee Chair.

In the future, I want to work fulltime as a web developer or software engineer. I hope to live in a city where I can see or play live music.
